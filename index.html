Online Security

In my previous occupation, I managed a multi-doctor and multi-specialty private medical practice. I was also in charge of the technical department and incorporating new technology and medical software. As our practice began to transition away from paper to a digital workflow, I found myself frequently coming across online security and HIPAA level compliance regarding storage of patient medical records, insurance claim information and communication with other organizations containing patient sensitive data. It is from this first hand experience that I began to see all the implications of online security and how it can swing from robust security to frailty depending on simple exploits and context.
I want to dedicate a page on my site to researching and learning about online security and how businesses are affected. I plan to organize online resources and provide notes and summaries of those resources.
